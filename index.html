About Us

Bihar Saraswati Seva Samiti is the leading non profit Organisation

Empowering Communities Through Service

Bihar Saraswati Seva Samiti, based in Virar West, Mumbai, is dedicated to fostering the spirit of unity, community, and cultural heritage. Our primary focus lies in organizing the Sarvajanik Saraswati Pooja, a celebration that brings together people from diverse backgrounds to honor the goddess of knowledge, wisdom, and art. Beyond this auspicious occasion, we are committed to various social initiatives aimed at uplifting the underprivileged and promoting education, health, and environmental sustainability.
