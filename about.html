Who We Are

A Non-Profit Devotional & Social Organisation

Bihar Saraswati Seva Samiti, based in Virar West, Mumbai, is a leading non-profit organisation dedicated to preserving cultural values and uplifting society through education, devotion and community service.

Our primary focus is organizing the Sarvajanik Saraswati Puja, a sacred celebration that unites people from diverse backgrounds in worship of Goddess Saraswati ‚Äî the symbol of knowledge, wisdom and art.

Our Mission

To promote education, cultural harmony and social welfare through devotional activities and community-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

To build a society rooted in knowledge, values and compassion, empowering future generations.

Our Values

Seva (Service), Sanskar (Values), Samarpan (Dedication) and Unity.
